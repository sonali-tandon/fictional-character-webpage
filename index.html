Sherlock Holmes

This is a fan page of fictional character Sherlock Holmes played by the actor Benedict Cumberbatch. The TV series - Sherlock Holmes is based on the mystery novels written by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. The genre is a combination of mystery, crime and comedy. Sherlock stays at the famous address 221 Baker Street, London. Sherlock is a private detective and genius who investigates crime by looking for clues and being super observant. He is somewhat of a mind reader.
