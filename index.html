Sherlock Holmes

This is a fan page of fictional character Sherlock Holmes played by the actor Benedict Cumberbatch. The TV series - Sherlock created by Mark Gatiss and Steven Moffat, is based on the mystery novels written by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. The genre is a combination of mystery, crime and comedy. Sherlock stays at the famous address 221 Baker Street, London. Sherlock is a private detective and genius who investigates crime by looking for clues and being super observant. He is somewhat of a mind reader.

Why I admire this character?

  • I relate to some parts of Sherlock's personality. Just like him, I have this strong urge of spending most of my time alone in my own company, just thinking about stuff. And even I have very few friends just like he does.
  • I like the mystery genre in general and all the episodes are super exciting with a great plot twist. The story is well thought out.
  • Apart from the character Sherlock, I am a big fan of Benedict Cumberbatch as well and I think he is an amazing actor and does justice to any role he portrays, especially this one.
